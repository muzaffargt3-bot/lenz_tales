Our Story

At Lenz Tales, we believe that every emotion, every tear, and every smile has a story to tell. Based in the heart of Kochi, Kerala, our passion is rooted in capturing real, unfiltered moments that last a lifetime.

We are a team of dedicated storytellers, blending a cinematic approach with a dark, emotional, and elegant aesthetic. Whether it's the grandeur of a wedding or the intimate quietness of a couple's portrait, we bring your vision to life.
